ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Kiana. I am a traditional artist who uses gouache and watercolours to paint. I have used watercolours since I was a child and started using gouache in the last few years. I prefer to create using traditional mediums and making them better digitally. I use photoshop to edit my traditional works and have years of experience editing photos and art. I enjoy the process of fixing traditional mistakes or enhancing areas to make the art better! I also have experience in graphic design, logo making as well as website design and UI.



Work Experience

• 15+ years of traditional art. This includes watercolour, gouache, oils and acrylics, small amounts of sculpture and other mediums. • 5+ years of photoshop. This includes traditional art touchups, illustrations and animation.
• 3+ years of graphic design. This includes logo design and other graphics.
• 6+ years of HTML/css3. This includes most types of websites.
• 1 year of Blender
